Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting the most out of your SmartSplash™ Silicone Sink Splash Guard.

Will this stick to my sink?

SmartSplash™ adheres best to smooth, non-porous surfaces like granite, marble, quartz, polished wood, and stainless steel. It may struggle to stick to rough, unsealed stone or heavily textured tiles because the suction cups need a flat surface to seal.

How do I clean it?

It's incredibly easy to clean. Rinse it with warm soapy water after use or place it on the top rack of your dishwasher to sanitize. The silicone is heat-resistant and built to handle daily use.

Does it get in the way of my arms?

No. We use soft, flexible silicone so that if your arms brush against it—or you lean on it—it bends comfortably instead of digging into your skin like hard plastic guards.

Will it mold or start to smell?

Silicone is naturally non-porous and resistant to mold and mildew. It doesn't absorb water or odors. Just give it a quick wipe or rinse periodically to keep it fresh and spotless.

What happens if I bump it?

The suction cups are designed to hold firm against normal bumps and arm pressure. If you hit it very hard, it will flex rather than crack, and you can simply press it back down if any suction releases.

Can I move it between sinks?

Yes. Just peel up one edge to release the vacuum seal, then move it to another sink and press down again. It's perfect for switching between your kitchen, laundry, or utility sink.

Is it safe for kids and families?

Absolutely. The soft silicone has no sharp edges, making it safe for families with children who are learning to help with dishes. It helps keep little helpers dry and reduces the risk of slippery puddles on the floor.
